OUR HISTORY

Establishment 2020: Atelier was founded in 2020 by Navtesh Thakur. The vision was to establish a haven for fashion enthusiasts, combining artistry, elegance, and sustainability. Since its inception, Atelier has grown into a premier destination for those seeking refined style and conscious choices.

OUR VALUES

  1. Sustainability:

    We are committed to sustainable fashion, utilizing eco-friendly materials, reducing waste, and championing a circular fashion economy.

  2. Artistry:

    Atelier stands for the artistry in fashion, embracing creativity and innovation in every piece we create.

  3. Quality Excellence:

    Our dedication to quality excellence ensures that each garment reflects the highest standards of craftsmanship and durability.

  4. Ethical Integrity:

    Atelier adheres to strict ethical practices, partnering with manufacturers who share our commitment to fair labor conditions.

  5. Inclusivity:

    Atelier is a space for all. We celebrate diversity, fostering a community where everyone is valued, seen, and embraced.

  6. Customer Elegance:

    Our customers are our priority. Atelier is devoted to providing exceptional service and ensuring our customers feel elegantly satisfied with their experiences. Feel free to further customize these details to suit the brand identity of "Atelier."

OUR APPROACH

  1. Artisanal Craftsmanship: Each piece is a work of art, meticulously crafted to showcase the highest standards of quality and attention to detail.
  2. Timeless Elegance: Inspired by both the contemporary and the classic, our collections offer enduring pieces that transcend fleeting trends.
  3. Sustainable Excellence: Atelier is dedicated to minimizing our environmental footprint through the use of sustainable materials, ethical manufacturing, and eco-conscious packaging.
  4. Inclusivity: Celebrating diversity, our designs are inclusive and tailored to embrace the beauty of all backgrounds, shapes, and sizes.
